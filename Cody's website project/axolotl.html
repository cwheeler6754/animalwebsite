Axolotl

An axolotl is a mexican water salamander. They remain in water as they are fish reptiles. One important thing to know about the habitat is never to use gravel or pebbels as the substrate. Sand is the best since they are bottom feeders and sand will not impact them like gravel. Diet consists of nightcrawlers, shrimp pellets, or feeder fish. I fed shrimp pellets to Jeffery as they are less messy. Water will stay cloudy for a while until the sand settels. A house which they can hind in is important as they are not fans of light.

Jeffery
