Albino Leopard Gecko

Habitat

A Leopard Gecko habitat has three main areas. The first is a house for them to sleep in. A water and food dish are also essential. I included a ramp in my tank to help Walter absord the heat. The next area is a heat lamp. The heat bulbs stay on 24/7 while the daylight gets turned off at night and back on in the morning. Geckos are easy to take care of. Diet consists of mealworms, crickets, waxworms and regular access to clean water.

Walter

This is my Albino Leopard Gecko. I have had him for over a year and a half. I do not like reptiles until I adopted Walter. The reason I enjoy walter is he is not slimy like other lizards.
