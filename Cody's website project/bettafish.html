Betta Fish

A Betta fish is one of the easiet fish to take care of. Tank mates include aquatic frogs, nano shrimp, snails, and other bettas only when they are both females. The diet for betta fish and other tank mates are the betta pellets you can find anywhere. The tanks I use has a built in filter and light which are essential to bettas. Lights should be turned on during the day and off at night. A filter will make cleaning the tank easier as most of the gunk will get sucked up.

Habitat

I have a five gallon tank with one betta, an aquatic frog, and a small fish which I do not know the name of. I have another tank (not pictured) which includes a betta and another aquatic frog. I have had betta fish forever. My betta pictured is over 5 years old. Betta fish only live for 5 years so I am worried he is going to pass away. The betta not pictured is almost 8 months old as I got her as a baby and raised her. She was smaller than a dime and now she is full grown. I highly recommend everyone getting a betta fish as a pet!
